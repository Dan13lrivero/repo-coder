Professional Musician Services:

Services Offered:

Live Session Musician Services:

  • Performance as a session musician for live gigs, concerts, and events.
  • Versatility in various musical genres (Rock, Metal, Jazz, Blues, Classical, etc.).
  • Collaborative performance with other musicians to deliver high-quality live shows.

Recording Session Musician:

  • Professional guitar, bass, piano, drums and gutural vocals playing for studio recording sessions.
  • Work with various styles: Electric, Acoustic, Fingerstyle, etc.
  • Experience in recording for different formats (Albums, Singles, Commercials, Movie Scores).
  • Ability to record guitar, bass, piano, drums and gutural vocals parts remotely for projects worldwide.

Guitar Lessons (Beginner to Advanced):

  • One-on-one and group guitar lessons tailored to students' goals.
  • Covering various techniques: Strumming, Fingerpicking, Soloing, Chords, etc.
  • Flexible lesson plans focusing on building solid fundamentals and expanding creativity.
  • Exploration of different genres and styles, including Rock, Blues, Classical, Jazz, and Metal.

Music Theory Lessons for Beginners:

  • Introduction to basic music theory concepts (notes, scales, intervals, and chords).
  • Understanding rhythm, time signatures, and dynamics.
  • Practical application of theory to enhance musicianship and improvisation skills.
  • Tools for writing and composing music.

Practical Classes with Other Instruments:

  • Offering cross-instrumental lessons (drums, bass, keyboard) to enhance musical versatility.
  • Learning the basics of each instrument to understand how they contribute to the overall sound of a band or recording.
  • Group sessions with other musicians to foster collaboration and musical communication.

Home Studio Recording Knowledge (Basic Skills):

  • Guidance on setting up a basic home studio for recording.
  • Introduction to essential recording equipment: microphones, audio interfaces, DAWs (Digital Audio Workstations), and headphones.
  • Basic mixing and editing techniques for creating polished demos and recordings.
  • Tips for capturing high-quality sound on a budget, including microphone placement and room acoustics.
  • Step-by-step tutorials for recording guitar and vocals in a home studio.
